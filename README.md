# Desk_to_door_communication

This is a 2-part project. Its purpose is to establish basic communication beetween a person that is sitting inside the room at his desk and a visitor standing outside his rooms door. It allows the person in the room to display certain information (such as what he is doing, is he busy, can the person outside easily enter) at a screen outside near the doors, for the visitor. The visitor can also send certain information such as announcing who he is, to the person at the desk. Also displayed at a small screen.
Other functions are a webhosted camera view of the outside of the room, a RFID tag reader that displays certain information on the screen after a readout and also plays a song on the speakers.
